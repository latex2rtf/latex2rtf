\section{Dot chars}
\textbf{Lowercase}

First is an \.{a} and an \.{x} and an \.{o}.

For math here are $\dot{a}$ and an \dot{x} and an \dot{o}.

$$a_2=\dot{a}$$

$$x_2=\dot{x}$$

$$o_2=\dot{o}$$

\textbf{Uppercase}

First is an \.{A} and an \.{X} and an \.{O}.

For math here are $\dot{A}$ and an \dot{X} and an \dot{O}.

$$A_2=\dot{A}$$
$$X_2=\dot{X}$$
$$O_2=\dot{O}$$
